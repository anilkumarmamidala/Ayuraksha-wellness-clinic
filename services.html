Sujok Therapy

Natural Healing Through Hands & Feet

Sujok Therapy is a Korean healing method based on stimulating pressure points in the hands and feet that correspond to organs and body systems.

Method of Treatment

The word “Sujok” comes from the Korean words “Su” (Hand) and “Jok” (Foot). This therapy works by applying pressure, seeds, magnets, massage tools, or gentle stimulation to specific points on the hands and feet that correspond to different organs and body systems.

Sujok Therapy is simple, safe, non-invasive, and highly effective. It helps restore the balance of energy within the body, improve circulation, relax the nervous system, and encourage the body to heal naturally without harmful side effects.

Why Choose Sujok Therapy?

Sujok Therapy is known for its simplicity, quick results, and holistic approach to healing. It not only helps relieve physical discomfort but also improves mental calmness and emotional balance, helping individuals live healthier and happier lives naturally
